Online Voting System

The Online Voting System is a secure and efficient platform designed to enable voters to register and cast their votes online. This system ensures transparency and accessibility, making it easier for people to participate in elections from anywhere.

Features:

  • Voter registration and authentication.
  • Secure voting process with encryption.
  • Real-time results and analytics.
